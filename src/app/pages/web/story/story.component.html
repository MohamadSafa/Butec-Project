The Story

Embarking from its roots in vibrant Beirut in 1964, BUTEC has evolved into a regional institutionalized group specializing in four business lines: Engineering & Contracting, Electro-Mechanical Solutions, Facility Services, and Utility Services.

Actively seeking to unlock synergies, BUTEC empowers the delivery of high-value, holistic solutions relying on the latest innovations. With an impressive legacy spanning almost 60 years, BUTEC takes pride in a rich history of delivering innovative projects, underpinned by a firm commitment to safety best practices and integrity. Our diverse project track record caters to various industries, including oil and gas, power, water, infrastructure and industrial facilities. Headquartered in Dubai and Beirut, BUTEC has expanded its presence to 21 countries throughout the Middle East and Africa. Our greatest asset is our people, and we take pride in our team of 4,500 skilled experts who are ready for new challenges and continuous growth. Driven by a strong dedication and passion, they embody a robust Group culture and uphold high standards of quality.

At BUTEC, our success extends far beyond mere expansion; it’s about cultivating impactful partnerships and collaborations with the international market. We earn trust through proficiency, consistently pushing boundaries and exceeding industry expectations. Since the beginning, BUTEC has been driven by adaptability, diversity, resilience, and innovation, all guided by a singular vision: creating a better future in everything we do… Join us on this journey of excellence and innovation.

Milestones

1964

BUTEC, a specialised EPC company is founded in Lebanon.

1980

Establishment of the Dubai branch.

1982

Establishment of the Saudi Arabia branch.

2005

Establishment of the Algeria branch.

2006

Establishment of the Qatar branch.

2007

Establishment of the Abu Dhabi branch.

2008

The International Finance Corporation (IFC), a member of the World Bank Group, became an equity partner (16.67%) in BUTEC Group.

2010

Establishment of the Jordan branch.

2011

Diversification into Utility Services with the 1st Electricity Distribution contract for EDL Lebanon.

2021

Inauguration of the new headquarters in Dubai.

2021

Establishment of the Ivory Coast branch.

2022

Diversification into Electro-mechanical and Facility Management through the acquisition of 17 specialized Engie companies in Africa.
