Life at BUTEC

Discover the essence of corporate life at BUTEC, where a commitment to excellence, innovation, and professional development defines our workplace culture. Join us as we embody a corporate ethos that fosters growth, diversity, and collaborative success.

Our people

BUTEC boasts a workforce of 4,200 employees, including 370 highly qualified engineers and administrators. Our corporate culture, founded on mutual loyalty, is nurtured by management, fostering a genuine team spirit. This cohesion enables effective resolution of challenges in project execution, with accumulated experience invested in future endeavors. Our engineers, graduates of reputable universities, cover various disciplines, bringing extensive on-site experience and staying abreast of industry advancements through in-house initiatives and frequent training opportunities abroad.

Diversity and inclusion

Embracing diversity is integral to our ethos at BUTEC. We foster an inclusive workforce that celebrates various cultures and beliefs, recognizing that diversity fuels creativity, enriches our work environment, and drives greater achievements.

Corporate culture
