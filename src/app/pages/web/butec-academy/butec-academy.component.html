BUTEC Academy

In the ever-evolving landscape of professional development, the BUTEC Academy stands as a testament to our commitment to excellence.

Launched as a group-wide initiative, the academy aims to foster the continuous growth of our people, reinforcing the distinctive BUTEC way and culture. As we reflect on our journey, it becomes evident that the BUTEC Academy has become a cornerstone in our pursuit of knowledge and skills development. The BUTEC Academy was conceptualized with a clear vision, driven by several key objectives:

  • Leverage BUTEC knowledge and expertise to share the BUTEC way
  • Upscale internal capacity and enhance both technical and behavioural competencies
  • Optimise newcomers’ experience and reduce ramp-up period
  • Increase employees’ efficiency on the job and/or when changing job roles
  • Establish a solid foundation to support career progression

To date, more than 30 technical training modules, covering a wide range of topics, have been meticulously developed internally by our Subject Matter Experts. These experts underwent comprehensive training and coaching sessions by our dedicated learning team, focusing on different methodologies for designing training content as well as delivering training sessions. The shared effort and commitment to success resulted in the creation of interactive, in-person, and virtual training programs that adhere to international learning standards. The commitment of our people, coupled with the continuous evolution of our learning methodologies, positions us for ongoing success. The BUTEC Academy is not just about training programs, it’s a transformative experience that empowers our workforce, propelling us toward greater heights of excellence. Together, we continue to build the blocks and shape the future of BUTEC, one skill at a time!
